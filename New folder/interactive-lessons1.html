Interactive Lessons for Middle School (Grades 6-8)

Lesson 1: Nutrition Basics

Learn about the importance of a balanced diet, healthy eating habits, and the nutrients your body needs to stay energized and healthy.

This lesson helps you understand the importance of regular physical activity for your overall health and fitness.

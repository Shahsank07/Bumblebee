Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to integrate health education across various subjects, helping students gain essential knowledge about nutrition, mental health, physical health, and digital tools. This comprehensive approach ensures students are equipped to make informed decisions regarding their well-being.

Middle School (6-8) Curriculum

The Middle School (6-8) curriculum delves deeper into topics such as nutrition, fitness, and mental health awareness. Students explore the relationship between diet, physical activity, and emotional well-being. Lessons also include peer relationships, building resilience, and stress management techniques.

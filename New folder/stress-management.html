Stress Management

Stress is a natural part of life, but chronic stress can take a toll on your mental and physical health. Learn effective strategies to manage stress and maintain emotional balance.

Stress Management Techniques

  • Exercise regularly
  • Practice deep breathing exercises
  • Maintain a healthy work-life balance
  • Engage in hobbies and relaxation activities

Tips for Reducing Stress

Identify your stress triggers and develop a plan to address them. Prioritize self-care and seek support from friends, family, or professionals when needed.

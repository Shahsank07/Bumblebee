Understanding Nutrition: An Introduction
In this lesson, we will learn about the importance of balanced nutrition and the role it plays in maintaining a healthy lifestyle. Eating the right foods can help you feel more energized, maintain a healthy weight, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases.

The Food Pyramid: Building a Healthy Plate
The Food Pyramid is a guide to help you understand the different food groups and the proportions in which they should be included in your diet. A balanced diet includes foods from all the groups in the right amounts.
Key Food Groups
  • Grains - Provides energy through carbohydrates.
  • Fruits and Vegetables - Rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
  • Proteins - Builds and repairs body tissues.
  • Dairy - Provides calcium for strong bones.
  • Fats - Necessary for brain health and energy.

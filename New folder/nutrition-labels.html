Reading Nutrition Labels

Understanding nutrition labels is key to making informed food choices. Here’s how to decode them.

Key Components

Here’s what to look for on a nutrition label:

  • Serving Size: The amount of food the label refers to.
  • Calories: The energy provided by one serving.
  • Nutrients: Includes fats, carbs, proteins, vitamins, and minerals.
  • % Daily Value: Shows how much a nutrient contributes to your daily diet.

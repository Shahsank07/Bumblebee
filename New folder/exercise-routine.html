Exercise Routines

Regular exercise is essential for maintaining physical health. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, these routines will help you stay active and healthy.

Benefits of Exercise

  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Builds muscle strength and endurance
  • Enhances flexibility and balance
  • Boosts mental health and reduces stress

Sample Routine

Start with a 10-minute warm-up, followed by 30 minutes of cardio (e.g., running, cycling), and finish with 20 minutes of strength training (e.g., squats, push-ups).

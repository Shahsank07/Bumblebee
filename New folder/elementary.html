Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to integrate health education across various subjects, helping students gain essential knowledge about nutrition, mental health, physical health, and digital tools. This comprehensive approach ensures students are equipped to make informed decisions regarding their well-being.

Elementary School (K-5) Curriculum

In the Elementary School (K-5) curriculum, we focus on building a strong foundation of health education. Students learn about basic nutrition, the importance of physical activity, and early mental health practices such as mindfulness and self-care. Interactive and fun lessons help them develop healthy habits that can last a lifetime.

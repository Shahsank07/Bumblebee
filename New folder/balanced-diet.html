Balanced Diet

A balanced diet is essential for maintaining good health and preventing chronic diseases. It includes a variety of foods from all food groups in the right proportions.

Food Groups

A balanced diet includes foods from the following groups:

  • Fruits and Vegetables: Rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber.
  • Proteins: Includes lean meats, fish, eggs, beans, and nuts.
  • Carbohydrates: Whole grains like brown rice, oats, and whole wheat bread.
  • Dairy: Milk, yogurt, and cheese for calcium and protein.
  • Fats: Healthy fats from avocados, nuts, and olive oil.

Portion Control

Managing portion sizes is crucial for maintaining a healthy weight. Here are some tips:

  • Use smaller plates to control portion sizes.
  • Fill half your plate with vegetables and fruits.
  • Avoid eating directly from the package to prevent overeating.
